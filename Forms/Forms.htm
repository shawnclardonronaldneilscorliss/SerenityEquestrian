Registrations & Releases

What to bring to camp
  • Water and juice...lots of fluids!
  • Backpack
  • Hat
  • Suntan lotion
  • Jeans
  • Cotton shirt to wear...this keeps you cool and covered
  • Jacket...just in case
  • Proper Shoes (e.g. Hiking Boots)
  • Diary or disposable camera (with you name on it)
  • And lastly Healthy lunch snacks and drinks in reusable containers
