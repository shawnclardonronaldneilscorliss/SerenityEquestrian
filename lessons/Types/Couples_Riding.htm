Couples Riding

Try something New, get back out in Nature.
Running: July 27th - July 31st Monday to Friday

Couples riding was created so that husband & wife, mother & daughter, father & son, couples of all kinds can come together in nature and learn how to better communicate with each other and the horses.

Learn how body language effects everything & and everyone around you.
Come and enjoy nature at its finest.

All in a Safe, Friendly and Encouraging environment.
