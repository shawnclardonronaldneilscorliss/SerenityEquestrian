Women & Horses

July 18th - July 22nd

Monday to Friday
from 6:30-8:30


Cost $ 285

 
  • Learn ground skills in natural horsemanship.
  • Take time in nature to breath and relax
  • Feel how these magnificent animals help you become calm.
  • Be with an animal who could easily choose not to be with you but will give you every chance to become skilled at "speaking" equine.

Horses mirror us, they can also tell when we are being authentic. In this program we will start to work on trusting our heart and gut to give us direction instead of relying solely on our minds. We will work on using our energy and the slightest movement to direct the horse.

Animals and nature can be our greatest teachers.
