Learning to Drive Carts

Experience the Fun of Driving horses!‏
Running: August 18th - August 22nd Monday to Friday
Camp starts at 5:30 - 7:00
$170
It is important to learn some basics of driving horses so you can understand what the driven horse needs and expects from you. With the proper skills you can develop better communication with your horses, be more comfortable with the lines or reins, be a confident, competent and a safe driver.

Our classes are geared for the family, so anyone 9 years old and up to 100 may partake in this fun activity!
Come meet our Minis: Cookie, Littlefoot, Odin, Stanley,Dickens and have the time of your life!

All in a Safe, Friendly and Encouraging environment.
