Young Riders

Kids may enroll in this course if they have participated in past summer or easter camps.
Part 1: July 7-11th Monday to Friday
Part 2: August 25-29th Monday to Friday
Camp starts at 8:00 - 12:00.
Both weeks recommended as we work into the other.
$445 per week.
In these 2 camps the children work on understanding level 1 ground skills and riding without assistance.
Developing confidence in leadership & saftey around the horses.
Riding, grooming, catching, leading & problem solving on their own.

All in a Safe, Friendly and Encouraging environment.
