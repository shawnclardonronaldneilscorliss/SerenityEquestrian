Sarah Novak and Amanda Labodie are trainers with Go Dog Go K9 Sports.They bring with them many years of combined dog experience. We will be focusing on building communication and understanding between you and your canine partner. We will cover cliker training, trick training, obedience and agility. They will be providing the dogs for the camps which vary from well trained to not at all.This will be hands on learning for everyone. Be prepared to learn a lot, have lots of fun and be kissed by lots of great dogs.
