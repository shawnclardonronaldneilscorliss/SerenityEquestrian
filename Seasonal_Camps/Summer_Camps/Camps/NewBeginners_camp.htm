New Beginners

Beginners or riders who have never experienced natural horsemanship.
July 25th to July 29th
Monday to Friday    
From 5:00 - 6:30pm

$225

This 5 day camp will introduce you to natural horsemansip

  • Choosing the perfect partner
  • Letting the horse catch you
  • Grooming with awareness
  • Training horses from the ground
  • Riding bareback, balancing energy
  • Relaxation & awareness of your body
  • Setting boundries and assertiveness in leadership with your horse
 

All in a Safe, Friendly and Encouraging environment.
