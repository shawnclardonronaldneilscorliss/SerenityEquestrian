Moving Up!
Part I

July 18th - 21st
1/2 Day Camps 8:00-12:00
Experience Necessary
(Must have participated in Serenity classes or camps previously).
Cost $ 425
  • In this camp you will develop skills for:
    •  keeping the horse and rider safe out side the arena.
    • We will work in our playground.
    • Riders will develop skills that will enable them to be able to handle a horse independantly via:
      • communications between riders,
      • safety
      • and reading their horses emotions.
    • Riders will also work on developing their own sense of calm and see how their emotions and energy affect the horse.
  • Each rider will work at their own level of comfort.
  • * New - Introduction to Clicker Training for dogs & chickens. 

All in a Safe, Friendly and Encouraging environment.
