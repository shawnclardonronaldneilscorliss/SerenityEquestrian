Horse & Hound

The week long camp will be spent learning how to interact with fellow students and the animals living at Serenity.
August 11th-15th Monday - Friday
   
Full Day Camp 8:30-4:30
Ages 6 - 14 Years
All levels of experience welcomed
$525



Working with Horses you'll learn:
  • Natural Horsemanship skills on the ground and riding bareback
  • Reading horses emotions and body language
  • How your energy affects the horse
  • Games and grooming
  • Saddling and mounting

Working with Dogs you'll learn:
  • How to build communication and understanding between you and your canine partner.
  •  
      We will cover
    • cliker training
    • trick training
    • obedience
    • agility

 

  •  Certified Trainer Donna Macloclin.
